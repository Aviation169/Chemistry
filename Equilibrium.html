Equilibrium

In chemistry, equilibrium refers to a state where the rates of forward and reverse reactions are equal, resulting in no net change in the concentrations of reactants and products over time. This dynamic balance is crucial in understanding chemical reactions and their conditions, influencing everything from industrial processes to biological systems.

Redox Reactions

Redox reactions, short for reduction-oxidation reactions, are fundamental in chemistry where electrons are transferred between reactants. Reduction involves gaining electrons, while oxidation involves losing them. These reactions play a crucial role in various chemical processes, from energy generation in batteries to metabolism in living organisms, highlighting their significance in both natural and industrial contexts.
